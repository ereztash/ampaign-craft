In 2016, Shahar Chen and Assaf Melochna saw a major challenge: a growing skills gap was making it harder for teams to deliver fast, effective service, while companies were sitting on huge amounts of valuable data but had no easy way to use it to make service more efficient.

Aquant's agentic platform transforms data into practical insights, captures and scales expert knowledge, and makes AI deployment frictionless, all so every team member can make confident decisions, even in high-stakes situations.

Aquant helps companies that build, sell, and service complex equipment turn data into AI agents — so every team, in every department, can use the same intelligence layer to make smarter decisions, faster.

Key differentiators: trained on real-world service data refined by subject matter experts; persona-based guidance tailored to specific roles (contact center agents, field technicians, etc.); end-to-end agentic platform supporting prebuilt and custom agents; continuous self-learning capabilities; works offline in low-connectivity environments; built-in domain expertise editable by internal experts.

Customers: field technicians, call centers, customer self-service teams, and service leaders across industrial equipment, medical devices, manufacturing, food equipment, high-tech, electronics, and printing.
