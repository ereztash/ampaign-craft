**Owner:** Marketing

## 1. Core Positioning Statement

**For** Israeli SMBs (1–50 employees) running their own marketing,
**FunnelForge is** the Hebrew-first behavioral-science growth platform with a built-in CRM,
**that** turns owner data into self-correcting recommendations across differentiation, marketing, sales, pricing and retention,
**unlike** point tools (Jasper, Madgicx, Klaviyo) that solve one layer or global CRMs (HubSpot, monday, Pipedrive) that don't speak your language and don't think behaviorally,
**because** we own the data end-to-end and our 6 closed feedback loops compound every day with new users.

## 2. Anti-Positioning (What We Are NOT)

Equally important: be loud about what we're not. Anti-positioning saves discovery time and pre-empts misaligned leads.

| We are NOT | Because |
|---|---|
| **An enterprise CRM** | We don't have multi-region compliance, custom-object modeling, SOC 2 Type II yet. SMB 1–50 employees only. |
| **A pure AI copy tool** | If you only need ad copy, use Jasper or ChatGPT — we charge for the full system. |
| **A Meta Ads optimizer** | Madgicx is deeper on Meta-only; we're broader and Hebrew-native. |
| **An English-first product** | Hebrew is primary; English is secondary. We do not chase US market in 2026. |
| **An agency replacement for everyone** | Top-tier strategic agencies still beat us on bespoke positioning for unique brands. We replace the ₪3K–₪8K/mo "generalist" tier, not the strategic ₪25K+ tier. |
| **A no-AI option** | If you distrust AI for Hebrew copy entirely, we are not for you (yet). |

## 3. Segment-by-Segment Positioning

The same product gets framed differently to each ICP segment. **One product, five different value propositions.**

### Segment A: Solo Founder / Micro-SMB (1–5 employees, DIY today)

- **Their current stack:** Excel + Canva + ChatGPT + Meta Ads Manager (~₪200/mo + 8–15 hrs/week of owner time)
- **Their pain:** Lead leakage, no attribution, generic Hebrew from ChatGPT, hours lost to Meta Ads Manager.
- **Our pitch:** *"תפסיק להפסיד לידים ב-Excel. ב-99 ש"ח לחודש אתה מקבל CRM, AI בעברית, וקמפיינים שלומדים מה ממיר — בלי לשכור איש שיווק."*
- **CTA:** Free tier, 3 funnels, no credit card.
- **Channel:** Founder-led organic content (LinkedIn, Hebrew Twitter), YouTube tutorials.
- **Risk:** Trust gap with AI Hebrew. Mitigate with sample outputs visible before signup.

### Segment B: Growing SMB (5–20 employees, currently using freelancer + agency mix)

- **Their current stack:** ₪1,500/mo Hebrew copywriter + ₪3,000–₪8,000/mo retainer + Excel CRM
- **Their pain:** Cost stack adds up to ₪5K–₪10K/mo without clear ROI; data lives in agency's tools.
- **Our pitch:** *"החלף 3 ספקים בכלי אחד. CRM + קופי + מודעות בעברית. הדאטה שלך נשארת אצלך."*
- **CTA:** Pro tier ₪99/mo (or Business ₪249/mo with WhatsApp + Campaign Cockpit).
- **Channel:** Outbound to LinkedIn/agency-using SMBs; case studies; consultant-reseller program.
- **Risk:** "I trust my freelancer" — counter with output samples, behavioral framework explanation.

### Segment C: Israeli SMB Already on a CRM (Powerlink, Monday, HubSpot)

- **Their current stack:** ₪200–₪1,500/mo on a CRM, plus separate copywriter, plus separate ads manager.
- **Their pain:** CRM doesn't generate copy; copy doesn't talk to CRM; ads don't loop back.
- **Our pitch:** *"ה-CRM שלך עובד עם הדאטה — FunnelForge עובדת עם הדאטה ועושה איתה משהו. החלף, או הריץ במקביל."*
- **CTA:** Migration path with CSV import; 14-day Pro trial.
- **Channel:** Comparison content (`/why-not-hubspot`, `/why-not-powerlink`), G2 IL listings, paid SEO.
- **Risk:** Switching cost. Mitigate with parallel-run mode (we read from their CRM during pilot).

### Segment D: Marketing Consultants & Small Agencies (Resellers)

- **Their pain:** Limited scale (8 clients max), every deliverable is from-scratch labor.
- **Our pitch:** *"FunnelForge נותן לך מכפיל × 5 על כל לקוח. אתה ממשיך להיות היועץ — אנחנו הביצוע."*
- **CTA:** Consultant-reseller program (see `docs/consultant-reseller-program.md`); revenue share + co-branding.
- **Channel:** Direct outbound to LinkedIn ("יועץ שיווק", "סוכנות דיגיטל"); webinars in Hebrew.
- **Risk:** Cannibalization fear (consultants worry we'll go direct). Mitigate with explicit "consultants only" tier features.

### Segment E: Israeli Performance Marketers Spending on Meta (Today on Madgicx)

- **Their pain:** Madgicx optimizes Meta but doesn't talk to CRM; Hebrew creative needs separate workflow.
- **Our pitch:** *"Madgicx ממקסם את ה-ROAS על Meta — אבל הוא לא יודע מי הלקוח. FunnelForge יודע, ובעברית."*
- **CTA:** "Triad demo" — connect Meta + import CRM + watch one campaign loop.
- **Channel:** Performance-marketing Hebrew Facebook groups, Madgicx alumni on LinkedIn.
- **Risk:** Madgicx loop on Meta is technically deeper. Counter with "we're full-funnel + Hebrew, they're Meta-only + English."

## 6. Objection Pre-Empts

For sales, support, and landing FAQ.

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "AI in Hebrew sounds robotic" | Show 3 sample outputs side-by-side: ChatGPT raw vs FunnelForge with stylome + behavioral framework. |
| "I already have a CRM" | Run parallel for 14 days — we read from yours. Decide based on data after 2 weeks. |
| "₪249/mo Business is expensive" | Show calculator: average Israeli SMB spends ₪5K–₪10K/mo on agency + freelancer + tools. We replace 3–4 line items. |
| "How is this different from Powerlink?" | Powerlink is a CRM with AI bolted on. We're a behavioral-science platform with a CRM underneath. The 6 closed feedback loops are the difference. |
| "What about HubSpot's free tier?" | HubSpot's CRM is English-only and broken in Hebrew. Try ours free for 3 funnels. Then compare. |
| "What if you go out of business?" | Daily CSV export of all CRM data; data is yours; no lock-in. Documented in `docs/security`. |
