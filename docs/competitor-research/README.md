## TL;DR — Three Findings

1. **Powerlink/Fireberry is the highest strategic threat.** Hebrew-native CRM with active AI investment and journey orchestration. Estimated 12–24 months to close the gap on FunnelForge's MOAT thesis. Counter-strategy: ship Wedge 5 (Triad in One Platform) before Q3 2026.
2. **The empty quadrant is "Hebrew + behavioral-science depth."** No competitor combines both. Powerlink is high-Hebrew, low-behavioral. HubSpot/monday/Madgicx/Klaviyo are mid-low-Hebrew. **FunnelForge owns the top-right quadrant by definition.**
3. **The real fight is not against global SaaS — it's against the ₪200/mo DIY stack and the ₪3K–₪8K/mo agency/freelancer market.** Our pricing is 4–50× cheaper than human alternatives and the trust-gap on AI Hebrew is the single biggest GTM risk.

## Methodology Caveats

This v1 was built from web research (T2/T3/T4 sources). Two of the four research agents had no live web access and worked from training-data knowledge through January 2026. **Specific pricing, employee counts, and recent funding figures should be re-verified before being used in investor materials.** The strategic structure (positioning, MOAT, expansion risk) is more stable.
