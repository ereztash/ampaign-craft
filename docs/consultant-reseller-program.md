# Consultant Reseller Program — Founding Partners

**Version 1.0 — April 2026**

---

## Overview

FunnelForge's Consultant Reseller Program turns Israel's ~12,000 independent marketing consultants and freelancers into distribution partners. Founding partners get exceptional economics, co-branded tools, and direct product influence — in exchange for evangelism, client adoption, and usage feedback.

---

## Why Consultants?

Marketing consultants are FunnelForge's highest-leverage distribution channel:

- **Trust is pre-built**: A consultant recommending a tool to their client converts at 3–5× the rate of cold outbound.
- **Built-in use case**: Consultants need exactly what FunnelForge provides — systematic strategy, bilingual copy, pricing frameworks, retention planning — for every client engagement.
- **Scale multiplier**: Each consultant brings 5–20 clients. 100 consultants = 500–2,000 end businesses served.
- **Feedback loop**: Consultants are sophisticated users who surface edge cases, missing features, and localization gaps faster than any other segment.

## Program Tiers

### Tier 1 — Founding Partner (first 100 partners, until capacity closes)

**Requirements:**
- Active marketing/business-growth practice (min. 5 clients/year)
- Commit to onboarding at least 3 clients to FunnelForge within 90 days
- Participate in 1 quarterly feedback session

**Benefits:**
- **30% recurring revenue share** on all clients referred (lifetime, not time-limited)
- **Founding Partner badge** on public consultant directory (coming Q3 2026)
- **White-label reports**: Client-facing PDF exports carry your branding, not FunnelForge
- **Dedicated Business tier access** for your own practice at no charge
- **Priority feature requests**: Founding partners' feature requests go into the top-10 queue for the next quarter
- **Early access**: Beta features 4 weeks ahead of general availability
- **Co-marketing**: Joint LinkedIn/newsletter mentions, case study co-authorship

**Revenue share mechanics:**
- Attribution: UTM-tagged referral link or coupon code
- Payout: Monthly via bank transfer (IL bank) or PayPal, minimum ₪100
- Chargeback protection: Refunds in the first 14 days do not trigger clawback on previously paid commissions
- Cap: None. There is no revenue share cap or tier-out provision for Founding Partners

### Tier 2 — Active Partner

**Requirements:**
- Referred at least 3 paying clients (Pro or Business tier)
- Active in the last 6 months

**Benefits:**
- **20% recurring revenue share**
- White-label reports
- Pro tier access for your practice at 50% off

### Tier 3 — Registered Referrer

**Requirements:**
- Signed up for the program

**Benefits:**
- **10% recurring revenue share** (first 12 months per client, then 5%)
- Pro tier access at 30% off

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do clients know they're using FunnelForge?**
White-label mode hides the FunnelForge watermark from PDF exports, but the product itself (app URLs, login) still shows FunnelForge branding. Full white-labeling (custom domain) is on the roadmap for H2 2026.

**Can I refer other consultants?**
Not in v1 of the program. Multi-level referral introduces compliance complexity. We may add a "Partner-of-Partner" 5% second-level share in a future version.

**What if a client I referred upgrades from Pro to Business?**
Your revenue share increases automatically to 30% of the new monthly amount. No action needed.

**What if a client I referred cancels?**
Revenue share stops on the month after cancellation. If they resubscribe within 12 months using your link, the share restarts.

**Is there an exclusivity clause?**
No. You're free to use, recommend, or resell any other tools. We don't require exclusivity.
